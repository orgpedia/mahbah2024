The Proposal: -
To implement special programmes for the development of "Goan" community belonging to Special Backward Classes on the lines of ongoing schemes for Scheduled Tribes by the Tribal Development Department. The decision has been taken in the cabinet meeting dated 29.04.2025.
Accordingly, approximately 6000 students belonging to the "Gowari" community belonging to the Special Backward Classes are provided education from Class I to Class XII in reputed residential schools of English medium every year through Reference No. 2 hereby approved by the Government Decision dated 14.05.2025.
Accordingly, the Director, Directorate of Other Backward Classes Bahujan Welfare, Maharashtra State, Pune has issued a notification in this regard vide Notification No. In a letter dated 15.05.2026 dated 4, the Wind Education Institute, Bhandara has submitted a proposal to the Government with prescribed forms (total score obtained by the school for the year 2026-2027 81) to approve the admission of Goan community students to Little Flowers English School, Gondia by selecting the school under the said scheme. Accordingly, it was under consideration of the Government to select Little Flambour's English School, Gondia, run by Pavan Shikshan Sansthan, Bhandara, for admission of Goan community students to a reputed English medium residential school in the city, subject to the terms and conditions of the relevant Government decision for admission of 100 students from the Gobari community belonging to Special Backward Classes for the academic year 2026-2027.
